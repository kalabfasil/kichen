Our Story

Bringing Family Recipes to Your Table

Founded by and Solomon in 2016, Habesha's Kitchen started as a small family restaurant sharing the authentic tastes of Ethiopia and Eritrea with our community. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, preserving the rich culinary heritage of the Horn of Africa.

We source our ingredients carefully, using traditional spices like berbere, mitmita, and niter kibbeh to create dishes that are both authentic and adapted to delight every palate. Every meal is served with our signature injera, made fresh daily.
